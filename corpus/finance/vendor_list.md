## Procurement Process for New Vendors

New vendor relationships over $5,000 annually require:

1. **Justification:** Brief written justification including alternatives evaluated
2. **Security review:** For SaaS vendors, completion of Acme's Vendor Security Questionnaire (VSQ)
3. **Legal review:** Contract review by Legal (typically 5-10 business days)
4. **Finance approval:** Sign-off from FP&A on budget alignment
5. **Procurement processing:** Final purchase order issuance

Total turnaround from request to active vendor is typically 3-4 weeks. For urgent needs, expedited review can be requested but requires VP sponsorship.
