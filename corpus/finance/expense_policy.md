# Travel and Expense Policy

All Acme Robotics employees are expected to spend company funds responsibly. This policy outlines what is reimbursable, what requires pre-approval, and how to submit expenses.

## General Principles

Use good judgment. Spend as if it were your own money — but not so frugally that you compromise your effectiveness on the job. When in doubt, ask your manager before incurring an expense.

All expenses must be submitted via Ramp within 30 days of the transaction date. Receipts are required for any single transaction over $25.

## Travel

### Flights

Domestic flights should be booked in economy class. Business class is permitted for international flights longer than 6 hours, or for any flight where the employee has a documented medical accommodation.

Flights should be booked through Acme's preferred booking tool (TripActions) at least 14 days in advance whenever possible.

### Hotels

The nightly hotel cap is $250 in Tier 1 cities (NYC, SF, London, Tokyo) and $180 elsewhere. Exceptions require pre-approval from a manager.

### Meals

Meal per diems while traveling:

- Breakfast: $20
- Lunch: $30
- Dinner: $60

These caps include tax and tip. Alcohol is reimbursable up to $25 per person per meal.

## Software and Subscriptions

Individual software subscriptions under $50/month do not require pre-approval but must be submitted in Ramp with a brief business justification. Subscriptions over $50/month or annual subscriptions over $500 require manager approval before purchase.

## Customer Entertainment

Customer dinners and events are reimbursable. For dinners with customers, the cap is $150 per person including tax and tip. For events (concert tickets, sporting events), pre-approval from a director or VP is required regardless of cost.

## Home Office Stipend

Remote employees receive a one-time $750 home office stipend within their first 60 days. The stipend can be used for a monitor, desk chair, headphones, or other ergonomic equipment.
