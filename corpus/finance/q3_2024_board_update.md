# Q3 2024 Board Update — CONFIDENTIAL

**Distribution restricted to: Board of Directors and Executive Staff**

This memo summarizes Acme Robotics' financial and operational performance for Q3 2024 (July 1 – September 30, 2024).

## Financial Highlights

- Q3 revenue: $58.4M, up 22% year-over-year
- Q3 gross margin: 64% (up from 61% in Q2)
- Q3 net loss: $(11.2M), compared to $(14.8M) in Q3 2023
- Cash position as of September 30: $187M
- Implied runway at current burn: 26 months

The Northwind Logistics partnership announced in September is expected to begin contributing meaningful ARR in Q1 2025, with full ramp by Q3 2025.

## Operational Highlights

- WX-7 fleet in production: 4,820 robots (up from 4,210 at end of Q2)
- New customer deployments in Q3: 7 (4 in US, 2 in Canada, 1 in Germany)
- Customer churn (ARR-weighted): 2.1% (best quarterly result in 18 months)
- Engineering headcount: 312 (target was 320; we are behind plan on senior hiring)

## Risks and Concerns

1. **Component supply.** Our LIDAR supplier in Munich is experiencing capacity constraints. We have placed advance orders to cover Q1 2025 but Q2 supply is uncertain.

2. **Competitive pressure.** Symbotic continues to win bids in our target segment. We are losing approximately 1 in 4 head-to-head deals on price, though our reliability story is winning longer-term renewals.

3. **Talent.** We are at 97% of plan on engineering hires but only 78% on hardware and embedded roles. The Krakow office is performing well; the Toronto office is struggling to compete with larger employers.

## Asks

1. Approval to proceed with the planned acquisition of FlexPick Software (separate memo distributed)
2. Approval of the FY25 operating plan, to be presented at the October board meeting
3. Discussion: should we accelerate the Series E timeline given current cash position?
