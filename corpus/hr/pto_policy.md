# Paid Time Off Policy

## Overview

Acme Robotics operates on a flexible PTO policy for full-time salaried employees. This policy applies to US-based employees; international employees should refer to their country-specific addendum.

## Vacation Time

Salaried full-time employees are eligible for unlimited PTO, with a minimum expected usage of 15 business days per calendar year. Managers are responsible for ensuring their direct reports meet the minimum.

PTO requests should be submitted in BambooHR at least two weeks in advance for absences of three or more business days.

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is separate from PTO and is not capped. Employees do not need to use PTO when ill. For absences exceeding three consecutive days, a doctor's note may be requested per state regulations.

## Parental Leave

All birthing parents receive 16 weeks of fully-paid leave. Non-birthing parents receive 12 weeks of fully-paid leave. Leave may be taken any time within the first 12 months following the child's birth or adoption.

## Bereavement Leave

Up to 10 business days of paid bereavement leave is provided for the death of an immediate family member, including spouse, child, parent, sibling, grandparent, or in-law equivalent.

## Holidays

Acme observes 11 paid holidays annually. The current year's holiday calendar is published in BambooHR each January. Holidays include New Year's Day, MLK Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving and the day after, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, and one floating holiday.
