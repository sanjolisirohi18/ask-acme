# 2024 Compensation Bands — CONFIDENTIAL

**Access restricted to: HR Business Partners and People Operations**

This document contains the official compensation bands for all US-based engineering roles at Acme Robotics, effective January 1, 2024. Bands are reviewed annually by the People Operations team in partnership with Compensation Consulting Partners.

## Engineering Bands

| Level | Title | Base Salary Range | Equity Range |
|---|---|---|---|
| E3 | Software Engineer II | $138,000 – $178,000 | 0.02% – 0.04% |
| E4 | Senior Software Engineer | $172,000 – $228,000 | 0.04% – 0.08% |
| E5 | Staff Software Engineer | $215,000 – $290,000 | 0.10% – 0.18% |
| E6 | Senior Staff Engineer | $260,000 – $355,000 | 0.18% – 0.32% |
| E7 | Principal Engineer | $310,000 – $420,000 | 0.30% – 0.55% |

## Bonus Targets

- E3: 10% of base salary
- E4: 12% of base salary
- E5: 15% of base salary
- E6: 18% of base salary
- E7: 20% of base salary

## Geographic Adjustments

Salary bands above reflect Tier 1 cities (Austin, San Francisco, New York, Boston). Other locations receive a geographic adjustment:

- Tier 2 (Denver, Seattle, Toronto): -5%
- Tier 3 (Portland, Pittsburgh, remote US): -10%
- Tier 4 (international): -20% to -35% depending on country

## Off-Cycle Adjustments

Off-cycle compensation adjustments outside the annual review require VP-level approval and must be justified by one of the following: significant scope expansion, retention risk with a competing offer, or correction of a documented banding error.
