# New Hire Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Acme Robotics. This guide walks you through your first two weeks. Your manager and assigned onboarding buddy are your primary points of contact — never hesitate to ask questions.

## Day 1: Logistics

You'll receive a welcome email from IT on your start date with instructions to set up your laptop. Most engineers receive a MacBook Pro 16" by default; if you need a different machine, let your manager know.

Your @acmerobotics-fictional.example email is provisioned automatically. You should be able to log in by 10am Pacific on your first day.

Required first-day tasks:

1. Complete I-9 verification with HR (in-person or via remote verification)
2. Set up direct deposit in BambooHR
3. Enroll in benefits within 30 days (medical, dental, vision, 401k)
4. Complete the security awareness training in Lessonly

## Week 1: Orientation

Monday morning includes a 90-minute company orientation hosted by People Ops. Tuesday and Wednesday are dedicated to product training — even non-product roles attend the WX-7 product overview, which is taught by a member of the product team.

By Friday, you should have:

- Met your team (most teams have a Friday afternoon social)
- Completed your environment setup (depending on role)
- Identified an "introductory project" with your manager — something to ship within your first 30 days

## Week 2: Embedding

In week 2, your manager will introduce you to your tech lead and rotate you through 1:1s with key cross-functional partners. Use this time to understand who does what.

## Slack Channels to Join

- #general — company-wide announcements
- #new-hires — welcome channel
- #engineering — engineering-wide discussions
- #help-it — for IT and laptop issues
- Your team-specific channel (your manager will share)

## Office Hours

CEO Marcus Reyes holds open office hours every Friday at 3pm Central. Anyone in the company can drop in. He also responds to direct Slack messages, usually within 24 hours.
