# About Acme Robotics

Acme Robotics is a fictional mid-sized robotics company headquartered in Austin, Texas. Founded in 2017, the company designs autonomous warehouse robots used by retail and logistics customers across North America and Europe.

## Mission

Acme's mission is to make warehouse automation accessible to mid-market companies that have historically been priced out of robotics solutions. The company's flagship product, the WX-7 picker robot, is deployed in over 200 facilities globally.

## Leadership

The company is led by CEO Marcus Reyes, who co-founded the firm with CTO Priya Vasquez. The current employee count is approximately 850, with engineering staff distributed across Austin, Toronto, and Krakow.

## Funding History

Acme has raised four rounds of venture funding totaling $312 million. The most recent Series D round closed in March 2024 at a valuation of $1.8 billion, led by Sequoia Capital with participation from Andreessen Horowitz and Tiger Global.
