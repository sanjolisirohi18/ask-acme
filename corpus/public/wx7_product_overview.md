# WX-7 Picker Robot Overview

The WX-7 is Acme Robotics' flagship autonomous picking robot, designed for mid-volume warehouse operations. It can navigate aisles up to 12 feet wide, pick items weighing up to 35 pounds, and operate continuously for 18 hours on a single charge.

## Key Specifications

- Maximum payload: 35 lbs (15.9 kg)
- Operating speed: up to 4.5 mph (7.2 km/h)
- Battery life: 18 hours nominal, 14 hours under heavy load
- Charging time: 90 minutes to 80%, 3 hours to full
- Operating temperature range: 35°F to 95°F (1.6°C to 35°C)
- Onboard compute: NVIDIA Jetson Orin NX with 16 GB unified memory
- Sensors: 8x RGB cameras, 2x LIDAR units, 6x ultrasonic proximity sensors

## Use Cases

The WX-7 is primarily deployed in e-commerce fulfillment centers, grocery distribution, and pharmaceutical warehousing. A typical deployment includes between 12 and 80 robots per facility, coordinated by Acme's Fleet Manager software.

## Pricing

The WX-7 is sold through enterprise agreements only. Customers typically pay a per-robot subscription fee that includes hardware, software updates, and 24/7 support. Pricing starts at $4,200 per robot per month for fleets of 20 or more.

## Compatibility

The WX-7 integrates with most warehouse management systems via Acme's WMS Connector, including SAP EWM, Manhattan Active, Blue Yonder, and Oracle WMS Cloud.
