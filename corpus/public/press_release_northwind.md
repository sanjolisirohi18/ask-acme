# Press Release: Acme Robotics Partners with Northwind Logistics

**Austin, TX — September 12, 2024** — Acme Robotics today announced a multi-year partnership with Northwind Logistics, one of North America's largest third-party logistics providers, to deploy 1,200 WX-7 picker robots across 14 Northwind facilities by the end of 2025.

The deployment represents Acme's largest single customer contract to date and is expected to generate approximately $58 million in recurring annual revenue.

"Northwind has been seeking a robotics partner that could match our pace of growth without compromising on reliability," said Northwind COO Janelle Park. "Acme's track record at scale and their willingness to embed engineers on-site during deployment made the choice straightforward."

The first phase of deployment will begin in November 2024 at Northwind's Dallas and Reno facilities. Acme will dedicate a team of 18 deployment engineers and customer success staff to the rollout.
