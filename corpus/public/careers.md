# Careers at Acme Robotics

Acme Robotics is hiring across engineering, operations, and customer success. We're a remote-friendly company with hubs in Austin, Toronto, and Krakow.

## Open Roles

We are currently hiring for the following positions:

- Senior Backend Engineer (Austin or Remote)
- Robotics Perception Engineer (Austin)
- Site Reliability Engineer (Remote)
- Customer Success Manager (Remote, EMEA)
- Hardware QA Engineer (Krakow)
- Technical Writer (Remote)

## Benefits

Full-time employees receive comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance, a 401(k) with 4% match, unlimited PTO (with a 3-week minimum), and an annual learning budget of $2,500.

## Interview Process

Our standard engineering interview loop consists of four stages: a recruiter screen, a technical phone screen, a take-home project (under 4 hours), and a virtual on-site with three interviews. We aim to give a final decision within 10 business days of the on-site.
