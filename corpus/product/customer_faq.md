# Customer FAQ

This FAQ addresses the most common questions from prospective and current customers. It is maintained by the Customer Success team and updated monthly.

## Deployment and Setup

**How long does a typical deployment take?**

For a new customer, the average time from contract signing to first robot in production is 10-12 weeks. This includes site survey, facility mapping, network setup, robot delivery, and operator training. Our 2025 goal is to reduce this to 4 weeks for standard deployments.

**Do we need to modify our warehouse?**

In most cases, no. WX-7 robots are designed to operate in standard warehouse environments without requiring infrastructure changes. We do require WiFi 6 coverage throughout the operating area and access to a power source for charging stations.

**What about our existing WMS?**

The WX-7 integrates with all major warehouse management systems via Acme's WMS Connector. Supported systems include SAP EWM, Manhattan Active, Blue Yonder, Oracle WMS Cloud, and several mid-market systems. For unsupported WMS, our solutions team can build a custom integration in 4-6 weeks.

## Operations

**What happens if a robot encounters an unfamiliar obstacle?**

The robot will stop, log the obstacle, and request human intervention via the operator dashboard. Operators typically respond within 2-3 minutes. The obstacle is added to the facility's known-obstacle map so future robots avoid the area.

**Can robots work alongside human pickers?**

Yes. The WX-7 is designed to operate safely in shared environments. Our safety system detects humans within 8 feet and reduces speed accordingly. Robots will stop entirely if a human enters the immediate path.

**What is the typical pick accuracy?**

Across our customer base, WX-7 pick accuracy averages 99.4%. Errors typically come from items being misplaced in incorrect bin locations, not from robot identification errors.

## Pricing and Contracts

**How is pricing structured?**

Pricing is based on a per-robot subscription model that includes hardware, software updates, and 24/7 support. Pricing starts at $4,200 per robot per month for fleets of 20 or more. Discounts are available for multi-year commitments and larger fleet sizes.

**Are there setup fees?**

For standard deployments, no setup fees apply for fleets over 30 robots. Smaller deployments may have a one-time integration fee depending on the complexity of the WMS integration.

## Support

**What's included in support?**

24/7 technical support, software updates, predictive maintenance alerts, and remote diagnostics. Hardware repairs are included for the first three years; after that, repairs are offered at cost.

**How do we reach support?**

Email support@acmerobotics-fictional.example or call the support hotline available in your operator portal. For urgent issues (production-blocking), use the in-portal "Emergency Support" button to page our on-call engineer directly.
